Scoring Guidelines 打分指引

Please read the following scoring criteria first. You will rate each video demo given below (i.e., the difference between the video of different methods and the reference face and video) from 1 to 5 points. The higher the score, the better the quality and the stronger the synchronization.
请您先阅读以下评分标准，你将对以下给出的每一个视频demo（即对比不同方法的视频和参考面部和参考视频的差异）做出1-5分的打分，分数越高表示质量越好，同步性越强。
