Scoring Guidelines 打分指引

Please read the following scoring criteria first. You will rate each voice demo given below (i.e., the difference between the speech of different methods and the reference voice) from 1 to 5 points. The higher the score, the better the quality and the stronger the synchronization.
请您先阅读以下评分标准，你将对以下给出的每一个音频demo（即对比不同方法的音频和真实音频的差异）做出1-5分的打分，分数越高表示质量越好，声色越相似。
